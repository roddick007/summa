Lokesh Kanagaraj

Lokesh Kanagaraj (/loʊkeɪʃ kənəɡərɑːdʒ/; born 1986) is an Indian film director, screenwriter and producer who works in Tamil cinema. He started his career with a short film in the 2016 anthology Aviyal, he later directed his first feature film Maanagaram (2017). He created the Lokesh Cinematic Universe (LCU) franchise after the commercial success of Kaithi (2019), the franchise's first installment.
